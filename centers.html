Centers

Providing services that Enhance African development through research

Applied Microeconomics

Our Applied Microeconomics center focuses on individual and firm-level economic behavior, market dynamics, and policy impacts at the micro level. We conduct research on labor markets, household economics, industrial organization, and public economics to inform targeted policy interventions.

  • Labor market analysis
  • Household economic studies
  • Market structure analysis
  • Policy impact evaluation

Applied Macroeconomics

The Applied Macroeconomics center examines economy-wide phenomena including growth, inflation, unemployment, and fiscal and monetary policy. Our research provides insights for national economic planning and policy formulation across African economies.

  • Economic growth analysis
  • Monetary and fiscal policy research
  • International trade studies
  • Economic forecasting

Social Research

Our Social Research center investigates social issues affecting African communities, including education, health, poverty, and social inequality. We provide evidence-based insights for social policy development and implementation.

  • Education policy research
  • Health systems analysis
  • Poverty and inequality studies
  • Social welfare evaluation

Center for Training and Development

The Center for Training and Development builds capacity across Africa by providing training programs, workshops, and educational resources for researchers, policymakers, and development practitioners.

  • Research methodology training
  • Policy analysis workshops
  • Statistical analysis courses
  • Professional development programs

Center for Monitoring and Evaluation

Our Monitoring and Evaluation center specializes in designing and implementing robust M&E systems for development programs and policies. We help organizations measure impact and improve program effectiveness.

  • M&E system design
  • Impact evaluation
  • Performance measurement
  • Results-based management

Funding and Financial Support Center

The Funding and Financial Support Center assists researchers and organizations in securing funding for development research and provides financial management support for research projects and programs.

  • Grant writing assistance
  • Funding opportunity identification
  • Financial project management
  • Budget planning and oversight
