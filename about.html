About Us

Who we are

African Institute for
Development Research and Evaluation (AIDRE)

AIDRE is an Africa-led and Africa-owned non-governmental, not-for-profit and non-religious development focused organization with the aim to enhance the socio-economic development of Africa through evidence informed policy making and capacity building.

Advancing research excellence for public policy in Africa.

Vision

To be the leading organization in Africa providing independent policy research, evaluation and training to enhance Africa's socio-economic development

Mission

The objectives of AIDRE:

  • To undertake relevant research that would inform development policy and programmes in Africa
  • To support public policy oriented human resource development through capacity building.
  • To engage in national and international development oriented advocacy
  • To write, issue and publish development oriented reports, articles, periodicals, books, newspapers, position papers, opinion pieces, and other documents.
  • To undertake public education on important economic and social issues
  • To press for policy reforms that promote the economic development of the continent

Values

AIDRE and all its members, officers and/or employees, shall be governed by the following core values:

  • Mutual respect: recognizing the innate worth of all people and embracing their diversity.
  • Independence: Working autonomously devoid of any religious, political or racial ideologies.
  • Excellence: striving for the highest quality in all our work with the latest techniques and tools.
  • Transparency: Embracing openness in our work and communications with others.
  • Efficiency: commitment to manage our organisation in the most cost effective and efficient manner.
  • Integrity: Our word is our honour

PARTNERSHIPS

AIDRE builds partnerships with various stakeholders, which cuts across public and private institutions, as well as non-governmental organisations
